## Proposed Projects

We have already started building an **E-Triage System** for hospitals.  
The system enables pre-screening of patients to manage workloads for:

- Hospital administrators  
- Health offices  
- Nurses  
- Doctors  

It functions as a **virtual patient management system**, leveraging **AI-powered prioritization** based on patient symptoms.
